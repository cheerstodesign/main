Over 15 years of combined experience in graphic design, web design and front end development

Cheers to Design is the small business of a passionate freelance web designer, Vanessa Salinas. She is currently working full time as a UX/UI designer in Dallas, TX. But she loves to share her passion with small to mid size companies looking for a new or better user experience online or through print media.

For the first part of her career she built a solid foundation of print, layout and pre-press production working at a graphics company for almost 10 years. While there she was also designing and building websites for multiple clients. She eventually moved on to pursue and focus solely on her interests in web design.

Her skills feature UX research, collaboration, problem solving, wire framing, prototyping, interaction and UI design. She also has a keen interest in accepting feedback and using it to build better experiences for users.

She looks forward to working with you for your online, digital or print needs.
